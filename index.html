About Me

I'm a fullstack software developer with a background in the medical field, driven by a passion for solving problems creatively. My coding journey began with a desire to build intuitive web applications that improve people's lives — a natural extension of my years helping others in healthcare. I work primarily with the MERN stack, and what excites me most about coding is the challenge — the frustration of hitting roadblocks and the rewarding sense of accomplishment that comes with pushing through them. I'm always learning, whether it's mastering new technologies or refining what I know. When I'm not coding, you'll find me snowboarding or exploring new hiking trails.
